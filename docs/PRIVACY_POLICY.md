## 1. What this covers
Chudbox is a car-garage / build tracker at chudbox.com. It is **local-first**: without an account,
your data lives only in your browser. An **optional account** adds cloud sync, backup, and sharing.

## 2. What we collect, and why

| Data | When | Why (purpose) |
|---|---|---|
| **Email + password hash** | If you create an account | Authentication; account recovery |
| **Your garage data** (cars, mods, maintenance, mileage check-ins, to-dos, issues, notes — incl. optional **VIN** and **licence plate**) | You enter it | To provide the service (store/sync/show your garage) |
| **Uploaded images** | You upload them | To show your car photos |
| **IP address** | Every request | **Security / rate-limiting only** — not used to profile you |
| **Share-link view counts** | When someone opens your share link | A simple count for you, the sharer |
| **Display name + "show on shares" choice** | If you set it | To show you as the owner/seller on shares you opt to share it on |

We collect only what's needed, ask per purpose, and do **not** use pre-checked consents. We do not
knowingly collect sensitive personal information.

## 3. Cookies
We set **one essential session cookie** to keep you logged in. We run **no analytics, advertising, or
third-party tracking**, so there is no consent banner — but we disclose the cookie here. If we ever add
analytics, it will be off by default and ask first.

## 4. Where your data is processed (outside Quebec)
Chudbox runs on **Cloudflare** (United States — Workers, D1, Durable Objects, R2 storage) and sends
account email via **Resend** (United States). So your personal information is **stored and processed
outside Quebec/Canada**. Before using these providers we assessed the transfer and concluded the
information receives adequate protection, and we have data-processing agreements in place with each.
`[Confirm the EFVP + signed DPAs are on file — COMPLIANCE.md item 5.]`

## 5. Sharing
Share links are **opt-in, unguessable, and revocable**. By default a shared link shows a **curated**
public view (no prices, notes, VIN, plate, or contact info). You choose per link whether it's a
**Showcase** or a **For-Sale listing**; a listing exposes only what you opt into (price, VIN, plate,
your display name as seller). We do **not sell** your personal information or share it for advertising.

## 6. Your rights (Quebec Law 25)
You may **access**, **correct**, **delete**, **port** (export), and **withdraw consent** for your
personal information, and ask us to stop sharing it. To exercise any right, email **privacy@chudbox.com**
or use the in-app controls. We respond **within 30 days**.
- **Deletion:** "Delete account" in Settings **erases everything** — your account, your synced garage
  (Durable Object), and your images (R2). `[Backups may retain copies for up to [N days] before they
  age out.]`
- **Export:** Settings → Backup & data lets you export your garage at any time.

## 7. Retention
